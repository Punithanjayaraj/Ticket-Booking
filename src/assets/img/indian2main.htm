Planning to watch Kamal Haasan’s Indian 2? Here’s everything we know about the film

Published: Jun 13, 2024 02:00 PM IST  | 
3 min read
By Ananya Swaroop
Share this article

Indian 2 is a passion project that Kamal Haasan announced in 2017. Back then, the veteran star was supposed to return to the titular role in the film soon with backing from Sri Venkateswara Creations. Years passed, but the movie never saw the light of the day. The team finally managed to wrap up the shoot for Indian 2 in March 2024, and now Kamal Haasan’s movie is gearing up for a worldwide release. Indian 2 will hit the theatres soon and we have all the details about its release date, cast and more.

After Sri Venkateswara Creations opted out of Kamal Haasan’s movie due to inflated production costs, Lyca Productions and Red Giant Movies came on board to produce the sequel. S Shankar returned to direct the sequel of the vigilante action Tamil film with new additions to the cast. After several delays, the film will finally arrive in theatres soon for fans to watch. If you are interested in watching Kamal Haasan’s Indian 2 as well, here are the details about its release date, cast and more.

Indian 2: What is Kamal Haasan’s movie all about?

The release of the Tamil film has seen many hurdles before its massive release. In 2020, an accident killed some crew members of the film. Then came the COVID-19 pandemic, which further delayed the filming for two years until Red Giant Movies joined the team as co-producers. The film has been shot in Chennai, Rajahmundry, Bhopal, Gwalior, Tirupati, Vijayawada, Johannesburg and Taiwan.

What is the release of Indian 2?

Kamal Haasan’s movie is scheduled to release in theatres on 12 July 2024. Since the film’s final length is over six hours, the movie has been split into two parts. The makers aim to release Indian 3 in 2025.

Meet the cast of Indian 2

Kamal Haasan will reprise his role as Veerasekaran Senapathy/Indian, while Siddharth will be seen as Chitra Varadharajan. The film also stars Kajal Agarwal, Rakul Preet Singh, SJ Surya, Bobby Simha, Vivek, Priya Bhavani Shankar, Brahmanandam, Samuthirakani, Nedumudi Venu, Delhi Ganesh, Manobala, Jagan, Kalidas Jayaram, Gulshan Grover, Zakir Hussain, Piyush Mishra and Akhilendra Mishra in key roles.

What is the plot of the movie?

The story will revolve around Senapathy, an ex-freedom fighter turned vigilante. He is known to fight against corruption in India as seen in the previous movie. In this sequel to the 1996 film Indian, an ageing Senapathy will return from Hong Kong to help Chitra Varadharajan. Together they will expose corrupt politicians in the country by posting viral videos on the internet.

– Is Indian 2 coming out?
Yes, the film will be released in July 2024.

– What is the Indian 2 story?
It marks the return of Senapathy, who will take out corrupt politicians with the help of Chitra Varadharajan.
